Jamaica Vehicle Import Guide

Everything you need to know about importing vehicles from Japan

üö¢ Shipping Vehicles from Japan to Jamaica

Container Shipping Options

There are three main container sizes for shipping vehicles:

  • 20ft Container: ¬•150,000 - Fits 1-2 small cars
  • 40ft Container: ¬•160,000 - Fits 2-3 medium cars
  • 40ft High Cube: ¬•180,000 - Fits 3-4 vehicles including larger ones
üí° Tip: Shipping multiple vehicles in one container significantly reduces per-vehicle shipping costs.

Shipping Insurance

Insurance is mandatory and calculated at 1.5% of the vehicle's purchase price. This protects your investment during the 4-6 week transit from Japan to Jamaica.

Transit Time

  • From Japan to Kingston Port: 4-6 weeks
  • Customs clearance: 1-3 days (after payment)
  • Total time: Approximately 5-7 weeks from purchase to collection

üìú Import Rules & Age Restrictions

Vehicle Age Limits

Jamaica has strict age limits for imported vehicles:

  • Motor Cars (Gasoline/Diesel): Maximum 5 years from year of manufacture
  • Pick-up Trucks: Maximum 6 years from year of manufacture
  • Hybrid Cars: Maximum 5 years from year of manufacture
  • Electric Vehicles (EV): Maximum 3 years from year of manufacture
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Vehicles exceeding the age limit will be rejected by Jamaica Customs and cannot be imported. Always verify the manufacturing year before purchasing!

Required Documentation

  • Bill of Lading (original)
  • Commercial Invoice or Pro Forma Invoice
  • Import Declaration Form (C82)
  • Certificate of Title or Export Certificate from Japan
  • Vehicle Registration Document
  • Tax Compliance Certificate (TCC)
  • Valid ID (Passport or National ID)

Customs Clearance Process

  1. Port Arrival: Vehicle arrives at Kingston Port
  2. Documentation Review: Customs inspects all documents (1-2 days)
  3. Physical Inspection: Customs officers inspect vehicle
  4. Duty Assessment: Calculation of all taxes and duties
  5. Payment: Pay Import Duty, SCTA, and GCT
  6. Release: Vehicle released for registration (same day after payment)

üí° Pro Tips for Successful Import

  • Verify Age Limit: Always check the year of manufacture against Jamaica's age restrictions before purchasing
  • Budget for All Costs: Include purchase + shipping + insurance + duties + taxes + registration in your total budget
  • Complete Documentation: Missing or incorrect documents cause delays. Ensure everything is accurate before shipping
  • Use Reputable Dealers: Work with established Japanese exporters who understand Jamaica's requirements
  • Consider Hybrid/EV: Hybrid and electric vehicles have significantly lower tax rates (up to 70% savings on duties!)
  • Plan for Delays: Allow extra time for customs clearance, especially during peak seasons (December-January)
